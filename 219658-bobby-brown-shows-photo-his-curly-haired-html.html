Bobby Brown Shares Adorable Picture of Daughter Hendrix Swimming on Her Birthday

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Singer Bobby Brown shared a beautiful snapshot of his daughter, Hendrix who recently turned four. The doting father also penned sweet words to honor his baby girl.

Bobby Brown's youngest daughter, Hendrix Estelle Sheba recently turned four and the doting father celebrated his baby with a beautiful photo and some sweet words.

Bobby Brown at the Affordable Old School Concert on July 4, 2015. | Photo: Getty Images

Taking to Instagram, Bobby shared an adorable picture of Hendrix in a blue swimsuit as she posed in a pool. Although Hendrix's body was submerged up to her shoulder in the water, her head, which was above water, saw a full display of her thick curly hair.

Holding on to the poolside pavement with one hand, Hendrix flashed a wide grin as she posed for the photo. In celebration of his daughter's birthday, Bobby took to the comments section where he wrote,

"Happy birthday, my beautiful princess daddy loves you so much."

Along with Bobby, several other fans and followers took to the comments section to leave sweet messages for the birthday girl. One user wrote, "Happy Birthday pretty girl, ?" while another sweetly added, "She's a beautiful princess,?????" with many others also gushing over Hendrix's cuteness.

Despite the controversies that have trailed his career and personal life, Bobby Brown has moved on from his past.

Bobby and his wife, Alicia Etheredge Brown welcomed Hendrix on July 21, 2016. In addition to her, Bobby and Alicia also share a son Cassius and daughter, Bodhi Jameson Rein.

Apart from his children with Alicia, Bobby is a father to four other kids, Landon, La'Princia, Bobby Jr., and the late Bobbi Kristina Brown whom he welcomed during his marriage to music icon, Whitney Houston.

Although Bobby and Whitney were one of the hottest couples in Hollywood at the time, the duo had a pretty rough marriage and in 2007, their union came to an end.

Despite the controversies that have trailed his career and personal life, Bobby has moved on from his past, and currently, it appears all he wants is to be a loving dad to his kids.
