Why did Selena Gomez unfollow Zayn Malik, Gigi Hadid, Bella Hadid &amp; Dua Lipa on Instagram?

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Selena Gomez has made a major move on social media that has fans wondering if she and Zayn Malik are over for good.

The pop star has unfollowed Zayn and several of his exes, including Gigi Hadid, Bella Hadid, Dua Lipa and Zendaya, on Instagram.

What does this mean for their rumored romance?

Selena and Zayn Fling

Selena and Zayn sparked dating rumors in March 2023, when they were reportedly seen kissing in New York City.

They went out in SoHo and looked very comfortable together. “It was clear that it was a date,” the source said.

However, neither of them confirmed or denied the rumors, and they never had another public sighting or photo together.

Selena spent the last two months in Paris, where she posted several TikTok videos declaring that she is “single” and “just a little high maintenance”.

Zayn, on the other hand, remained silent on his relationship status.

Selena Gomez Social Media Cleanse

Fans noticed that Selena quietly unfollowed Zayn on Instagram on June 25, 2023.

She also unfollowed his ex-girlfriend and mother of his daughter Khai, Gigi Hadid, as well as Gigi’s sister Bella Hadid, who also dated Zayn briefly in 2016.

In addition, Selena unfollowed Dua Lipa, who was linked to Zayn in 2017, and Zendaya, who co-starred with him in the movie “The Greatest Showman”.

Celebrity news fan account PopFaction pointed out Selena’s unfollow spree on Instagram, showing screenshots of her follower list before and after.

Zayn still follows Selena on Instagram, as do Gigi, Bella, Dua and Zendaya.

They seem to be busy with their own careers and personal lives.
