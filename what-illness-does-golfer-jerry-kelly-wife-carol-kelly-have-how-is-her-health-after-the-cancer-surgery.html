What Illness Does Golfer Jerry Kelly Wife Carol Kelly Have?

Carol Kelly, the wife of golfer Jerry Kelly, was suffering from kidney cancer.

Carol needed surgery as soon as possible because the illness was fatal, which is why she was not present to support her husband in the final event of the massive 2020-21 PGA Tour wraparound season.

The surgery was performed to remove her right kidney after doctors found a cancerous mass the size of her kidney.

The operation was carried out three weeks before the main event. Kelly had a great run in the tournament, but he couldn’t catch up to Charles Schwab for first place and had to withdraw early.

Following her exit, Kelly addressed the media, saying, “It’s so much easier knowing that I’m going home to see Carol now.” He added that leaving her and flying to compete was difficult for him, but Carol encouraged him to play.

Meanwhile, this is the first time that Jerry’s wife got diagnosed with cancer. Carol had been diagnosed with phase three melanoma in her leg beforehand.

Carol Kelly Cancer Surgery and Health Condition

The health condition of Carol Kelly appears to be in excellent shape following her malignant kidney removal surgery.

Carol’s cancer diagnosis is the second time she’s been diagnosed, so there’s a chance she’ll get sick again after some time.

However, right now, Carol and the whole Kelly family appear to be in excellent health at the moment.

All credit goes to the doctors and nurses who performed the successful surgery and eliminated the cancer-causing cells from her body.

Carol appears to be more active after the successful operation, despite what must have been a tough time for her during the surgery and before it.
