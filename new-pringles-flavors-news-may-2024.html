4 New Pringles Flavors: Everything We Know

July 2024 · 2 minute read

It seems like every time I go down the chip aisle there are three or four new Pringles flavors. Recently, Pringles released a Texas Brisket BBQ flavor, which we reviewed, but more Pringles are making their way to shelves now and we’ve got the details. Here’s everything you need to know about the four newest Pringles flavors.

Cheesy Jalapeño Popper Pringles

First up, we have Cheesy Jalapeño Popper, which, according to Markie_devo, is not exclusive to a specific store. Some of our favorite cheese puffs are jalapeño popper flavored, so we are optimistic about a cheesy Pringles version.

Carnitas Taco Pringles

Second up, we have Carnitas Taco described as “bursting with the savory flavor of pork carnitas and aromatic seasonings including onion, garlic, cumin, and oregano.” These will also not be exclusive to any specific stores. Pringles isn’t the first brand to distill the flavors of a taco into a chip (we are massive fans of the limited edition Crunchy Taco Lay’s that just dropped). But that doesn’t mean they won’t put their own spin to the concept. 

All-Dressed Pringles

Next up, we have formerly Canadian-exclusive (and limited time only) All Dressed (find out what “all dressed” flavoring is). All dressed snacks are very SUPER satisfying and I do not expect this to be any different. Glad that these are being given a chance outside of Canada! 

Minecraft Spicy TNT Pringles

Last but not least of the new Pringles flavors is a new limited edition Minecraft Pringles flavor (thankfully not those Suspicious Stew ones). This one is called Spicy TNT. According to Pringles, these crisps are “crafted with a blend of spicy flavors.” They’re also described as being “satisfyingly salty.” Pringles suggests you bring a can along for “video game time,” whatever that means. So far, these have only been spotted at Target, but I am not positive if they are exclusive or not.

Hats off to you, Mr. Pringles, aka Julius. There are a lot of really incredible snacks on the way and I personally can’t wait to try all of them. I tip my hat to you! (I am not wearing a hat, but use your imagination.)
