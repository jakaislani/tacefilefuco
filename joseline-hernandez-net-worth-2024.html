Early Life

Joseline Hernandez was born on November 3, 1986, in Ponce, Puerto Rico. She moved to Florida with her family when she was a teenager. Hernandez had a difficult childhood, with her father being absent and her mother struggling with drug addiction. Hernandez’s life took a turn when she was discovered by Grammy Award-winning producer Stevie J in an Atlanta strip club. He saw potential in her and helped her transition into the music industry. This was the beginning of her journey to stardom and wealth.

Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta

Her breakthrough came when she was cast in the VH1 reality TV show “Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta” in 2012. The show followed the lives of women involved in the southern capital’s hip-hop industry. Hernandez quickly became a fan favorite due to her outspoken nature and dramatic relationship with Stevie J.

Music Career

Aside from reality TV, Hernandez also pursued a career in music. She released several singles and music videos, including “Church,” “Stingy with My Kutty Katt,” and “Do It Like It’s My Bday.” Her music career contributed significantly to her net worth.

Joseline’s Cabaret

Acting Career

Personal Life
