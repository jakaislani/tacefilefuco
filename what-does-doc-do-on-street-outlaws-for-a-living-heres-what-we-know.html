James Love, who you’ll recognize best as Doc on TV, is a man who has fully exploited his passion for cars. He is part of the Discovery Channel reality series Off-the-Street where he races with The Street Beast; a custom 1970 Chevrolet Monte Carlo with which he became one of the deadliest racers in the series, earning big reward money.

The Outlaws show is what Doc has grown to be associated with over the years. His fans and anyone who has seen and heard of him have often wondered if he lived outside of street performance. We were initially clueless about it and decided to research it to be better informed and in turn educate others. Here we present everything we found out about “What does Street Outlaws Doc do for a living?”. But first, know its basics before you get popular with the TV show.

Basic information about Doc

  • He was born on February 19, 1969, in Moore, Oklahoma, and his name is James Love, the son of a concrete construction worker.
  • James was educated at Moore High School in Oklahoma.

  • Her first car, a Monte Carlo 77, her mother’s, was given to her so she could use it to pick up her younger brother and sister from school and walk to work.

  • Many years have passed and the OK (Oklahoma) man’s interest in cars has continued to soar despite life’s challenges.

  • This once poor child, who had a financially difficult childhood, has since used his passion for automobiles to transform his life forever.
  • Doc is one of the strongest hands on the wheels of The Outlaws show. His speed in races saw him well rewarded. The driver’s net worth, sources say, is over $500,000.

What does Doc do on ‘Street Outlaws’ for a living? Here’s what we know

After graduating from UTI, the guy returned to Oklahoma with the joy of racing cars. He bought his first race car, a 1970 Chevrolet Monte Carlo from eBay in Wisconsin, and drove it back to Oklahoma. Doc christened his Chevrolet “The Street Beast” and started racing with it in earnest.

Along with his interest in racing, Doc was also an auto mechanic at a garage where he used his knowledge and training in machinery to repair and customize cars brought into the garage. In 2001, he started his own repair shop called Southwest Diesel Service with his grandfather.

Doc still harbored ambitions to dedicate more time to racing his Chevrolet, however, with this passion seeing him become part of an American reality series called Off-the-Street in the year 2013. He modified his Street Beast to provide the best performance on the race tracks, as well as its ability and control over the aerodynamics of the vehicle. He’s done very well doing what he loves the most and he can’t seem to stop his dominance over the race.

Coming to the question of what Doc does for a living, he has a lot to do in furthering his career and being a father to his family members. In the world of street racing and probably every other racing sport, drivers see it as a full time job and most don’t do anything else outside of training and racing. Most guys in Off-the-Street actually quit their full-time jobs, but that’s not the same for Doc.

He has a repair shop that he started with his grandfather several years ago and still works there because it’s something special for him and not just a business. So basically what he does for a living is he’s an off-the-street racing driver as well as an auto mechanic.
