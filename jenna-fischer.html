The Office Actress Jenna Fischer Has Been in Marital Life Twice.

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Primetime Emmy Award nominee, Jenna Fischer is an American actress who came to fame after starring Pam Beesly on the NBC TV show, The Office. She was a producer in the last season (2012–2013) and it was aired from March 24, 2005, to May 16, 2013.

In 2010, she starred in and worked as an associate producer on the NBC drama, You, Me, and the Apocalypse. Apart from her professional career, she has tied the knot with two men. And she is the mother of two kids. Let's discuss this in detail.

Married Life With Current Husband, Lee Kirk

47 years old Jenna Fischer has been married two times throughout her entire life. As of now, she is in a loving and blissful marital relationship with her now-husband, Lee Kirk. He is a film director who is famous for directing The Giant Mechanical Man, in 2012, where she also starred. 

The couple first met back in 2008 and started dating after six or seven months. In June 2009, they announced their engagement while vacationing in Europe. Eventually, they tied the knot on 3rd July 2010 after three years of dating. 

The intimate ceremony was held in Malibu and Jeff Probst's officiated their day. Since then, the low-key couple shares a strong connection.

Together, they have two children, a son Weston Lee Kirk, born on September 24, 2011, and Harper Marie Kirk, born on May 25, 2014.

Jenna Fischer's First Marriage

Before Fischer and Kirk were an item, The Office star was previously married to James Gunn, a director and actor, who is known for directing the web series, James Gunn's PG Porn from 2008 to 2009.

They first met in 1999 after they were introduced through her childhood friend or Gunn’s brother, Sean Gunn. She and the Guardians of the Galaxy director got married on October 7, 2000, in a private ceremony.

However, Fischer and James split in September 2007 after seven years of marriage. And their divorce was finalized in 2008 and remained friends. Together, they had no kids. 

What is Jenna Fischer Net Worth?

As of early 2021, Jenna Fischer has an impressive net worth of $14 Million, as per Celebrity Net Worth. She has accumulated her fortune throughout her acting, producing and directing career.

Fischer earned $150,00 per episode or around $4 million per season by the end of the NBC sitcom The Office. Between 2005 and 2013, she starred as the main cast member in a total of 188 episodes. Additionally, she took guest roles in many TV shows such as Undeclared, Strong Medicine, Six Feet Under, You, Me and the Apocalypse, and more.

From 2018–2019, she was the main cast member of the ABC TV drama, Splitting Up Together, alongside Oliver Hudson, Lindsay Price, and Olivia Keville. On the other hand, her films credits are The 40-Year-Old Virgin, Slither, Blades of Glory, Hall Pass, and The 15:17 to Paris.

Jenna Fischer's Bio and Early Life

Jenna Fischer was born on 7th March 1974 in Fort Wayne, Indiana, the USA as Regina Marie Fischer. Her parents are James E. Fischer, an engineer and Anne Fischer, a history teacher. She has a younger sister named Emily Fischer, a teacher.

Fischer attended the Pierremont Elementary School in Missouri and then went to Nerinx Hall High School. Later she earned her bachelor’s degree in Arts from the Truman State University in theatre and minors in Journalism.
