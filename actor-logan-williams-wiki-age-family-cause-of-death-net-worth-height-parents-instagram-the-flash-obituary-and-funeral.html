Logan Williams Biography – Logan Williams Wiki

Logan Williams was a talented child actor. He was best known for his role as a young Barry Allen on the CW superhero series “The Flash.” He also appeared in “When Calls the Heart” with Lori Loughlin, as well as “Supernatural” and “The Whispers.”

He won best actor awards and a nomination as Best Newcomer during the UBCP/ACTRA awards in 2015, according to Tri-City News. The actor, who grew up in Coquitlam, attended Hope Lutheran school and Terry Fox secondary in Port Coquitlam.

Logan Williams Age

Logan Williams was born on April 9, 2003, in Vancouver, Canada. He died on April 2, 2020. He was 16 years.

Logan Williams Parents, Family

Logan Williams was the son of Marlyse Williams (mother) and Clive Williams (father). His mother is Swiss and his father is South African.

Logan Williams Death

Logan Williams died on Thursday, April 2, 2020, at the age of 16. His mother, Marlyse Williams, confirmed his death to The Tri-City News. She told TriCity News that the family is “absolutely devastated” by his death. Her sorrow is made worse by the fact that she cannot grieve for her son with her family because of the Coronavirus physical distancing restrictions. She said, “I am not able to hug my parents who lost their only grandchild. It’s hard. With his talent and gorgeous looks, Logan had the potential to be a huge star.”

Grant Gustin, who stars as the adult version of Barry Allen on the hit series, paid tribute to Williams in an Instagram post on Friday, April 3. He wrote, “Just hearing the devastating news that Logan Williams has passed away suddenly. This picture was early in the filming of The Flash pilot episode back in 2014. I was so impressed by not only Logan’s talent but his professionalism on set. My thoughts and prayers will be with him and his family during what is I’m sure an unimaginably difficult time for them. Please keep Logan and his family in your thoughts and prayers during what has been a strange and trying time for us all. Sending love to everyone. ❤️”

When Calls the Heart star Erin Krakow tweeted: “Heartbroken to learn of the passing of Logan Williams who played Miles Montgomery in several seasons of [When Calls the Heart].”

John Wesley Shipp wrote on Twitter, “Heartsick to learn of Logan Williams’ death at 16. He was 100% committed to playing young Barry Allen, and we missed him once we moved past that part of the story. Love and compassion to Logan’s family and friends in your grief.”

Logan Williams Cause of Death

Logan Williams’ cause of death was a fentanyl overdose. His mother, Marlyse Williams, told the New York Post that preliminary toxicology results revealed that he died of a fentanyl overdose. Williams told The Post, “His death is not going to be in vain. He’s going to help a lot of people down the road.”

Logan Williams Height

Logan Williams was 5 feet 2 inches (1.57 m) tall.
