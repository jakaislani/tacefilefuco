Best Yogurt Weve Found in Our Taste Tests

June 2024 · 3 minute read

The best yogurt is creamy and luscious. It’s great on its own or used as an ingredient in cooking and baking. It doesn’t matter if you’re looking for a special dietary yogurt (like the best keto yogurt or the best vegan yogurt) or if you’re looking for regional yogurt (like the best skyr Icelandic yogurt or best Greek yogurt)—they should all be delicious!

Here at Sporked, we have tried pretty much every type of yogurt you could possibly imagine. Now, we’ve taken all our knowledge and compiled it into this neat list so you can find the best yogurt, no matter your yogurt needs!

Best Plain

Trader Joe’s Greek Whole Milk Plain Yogurt

Not into sweet yogurt? This is the best plain yogurt! Trader Joe’s stocks my favorite plain yogurt (and one of the best Greek yogurt options) for cooking. This stuff is thick and rich and makes a great tzatziki.

Credit: Liv Averett

10/10

There are so many vanilla yogurts out there, but La Fermiere makes the best vanilla yogurt by far. Of course they do! They actually use vanilla beans, which makes this yogurt taste delicious and feel fancy.

Best Greek

Wallaby Aussie Greek Style Plain YogurtInstacart

The best Greek yogurt we found in our taste test was this Wallaby Aussie Greek Style Plain Yogurt. I didn’t realize Aussies were making such good yogurt. Sorry, y’all! This stuff is super thick and rich and would be great in sweet and savory preparations.

Best Flavored

Oikos Lemon Meringue Greek YogurtVons

We were stopped in our tracks by this Oikos Lemon Meringue Greek Yogurt. It’s easily the best flavored Greek yogurt we’ve ever tried! It literally tastes like a lemon meringue pie minus the crust, but you could always sprinkle it with some crushed-up graham crackers if you miss that crunch.

Credit: Liv Averett / Vons

Rating:

10/10

Best Kefir

Green Valley Creamery Organic Plain Whole Milk KefirSprouts

If you’re looking for a drinkable yogurt that has incredible health benefits, try out the best kefir. It’s packed with vitamins, calcium, and probiotics. This stuff is great by itself or used in a smoothie. Once you try the best kefir, you might just be a kefir convert forever.

Credit: Liv Averett / Sprouts

10/10

Best Probiotic

Stonyfield Organic Whole Milk Probiotic Vanilla YogurtTarget

Best Vegan

Cocojune Vanilla Chamomile Vegan YogurtInstacart

Vegans, rejoice! There are some very good vegan yogurt brands out there. The best vegan yogurt, however, is this Vanilla Chamomile flavor from Cocojune. It’s sweet and floral while still being subtle. It also is super creamy. I wouldn’t have known this was vegan if the container hadn’t told me.
